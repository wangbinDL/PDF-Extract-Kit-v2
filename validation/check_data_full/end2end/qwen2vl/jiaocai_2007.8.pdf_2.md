# 2007 AMC 8 Problems/Problem 3

## Problem
What is the sum of the two smallest prime factors of 250?

(A) 2 (B) 5 (C) 7 (D) 10 (E) 12

## Solution
We prime factor $250 = 2 \cdot 5^3$. The smallest two are 2 and 5. $2 + 5 = \boxed{7}$.
