## Problem 25
Let \(f: \mathbb{C} \rightarrow \mathbb{C}\) be defined by \(f(z) = z^2 + iz + 1\). How many complex numbers \(z\) are there such that \(\text{Im}(z) > 0\) and both the real and the imaginary parts of \(f(z)\) are integers with absolute value at most 10?

\(f\) 是定义在复数域上的一个函数，\(f: \mathbb{C} \rightarrow \mathbb{C}\)，且 \(f(z) = z^2 + iz + 1\)，有多少个这样的复数 \(z\) 存在，满足 \(\text{Im}(z) > 0\)（\(z\) 的虚部大于 0），且 \(f(z)\) 的实部和虚部都是绝对值至多为 10 的整数？

(A) 399    (B) 401    (C) 413    (D) 431    (E) 441
