\section*{Problem}
What is the sum of the two smallest prime factors of 250 ?
(A) 2
(B) 5
(C) 7
(D) 10
(E) 12
\section*{Solution}
We prime factor \(250=2 \cdot 5^{3}\). The smallest two are 2 and 5. \(2+5=0\)
