# 2007 Amc 8 Problems/Problem 3

Problem What is the sum of the two smallest prime factors of 250?

(A) 2
(C) 7
(D) 10
(B) 5
(E) 12 Solution We prime factor 250 = 2 · 53. The smallest two are 2 and 5. 2 + 5 = C
